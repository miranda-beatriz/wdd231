Site Name:

The Reading Room. I chose this name because it brings an idea of a comfortable and familiar place, which is my idea for this book club, to let those who visit the website feel that they want to participate in this club.

Site Purpose:

This website will serve as a place for book lovers to connect and engage in an online book club. The site will provide a submission section for joining the club, explain how the club works, highlight the benefits of being a member, and showcase the positive impacts of reading on personal growth. It aims to foster a love of reading, encourage meaningful discussions, and build a community of passionate readers.

Scenarios:

What type of books to the club read?
